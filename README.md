### **_Description_**

GA Shine Booth is a web app that lets users capture photos with live video, add fun stickers, and save or upload them to the cloud. Users can drag and drop stickers on their photos, see a countdown before taking pictures, and download or upload their creations for sharing. It’s your own personal photo booth — making memories more fun and interactive!

#### _*Capture, decorate, and share your moments — all with GA Shine Booth! 📸*_

---

### **_Technologies Used_**

- Node.js
- Express.js
- Cloudinary API for image upload
- JavaScript / HTML / CSS
- Canvas
- Multer

---

## **_Special Features_**

1. Live video preview from your camera
2. Countdown before capturing photos
3. Drag-and-drop stickers on photos
4. Download captured photos locally
5. Upload captured photos to Cloudinary
